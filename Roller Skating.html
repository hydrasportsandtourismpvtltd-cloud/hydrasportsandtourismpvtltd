About the Rink

Built for Balance,
Trained for Speed

Our Nilambur rink is part of HYDRA Sports & Tourism Pvt. Ltd., home to five Guinness World Records and a coaching philosophy built on discipline, patience, and individual attention.

Every skater — whether stepping onto wheels for the first time or chasing a state title — moves through a structured path: posture, balance, stride, and speed. Coaches track progress session by session, not just by the clock.

Skating here means joining a wider sports family, with pathways into Rollball and Aeroskateball for students who want to take their wheels further.

Programs

Pick Your Track

Every skater starts somewhere. Ours is a path, not a class — you move up when you're ready, not when the calendar says so.

01 · GETTING STARTED

Beginner

Balance, falling safely, first strides. For skaters aged 4+ with zero prior experience. Full protective gear guidance included.

02 · BUILDING SPEED

Intermediate

Stride efficiency, turns, backward skating and endurance drills. For skaters ready to move beyond the basics.

03 · COMPETITION READY

Advanced

Race technique, crossovers, and tournament conditioning for skaters targeting school, district and state meets.
