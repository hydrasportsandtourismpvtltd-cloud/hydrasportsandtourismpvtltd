About our roller skating academy

Structured skating coaching, built for real progress

Hydra Sports & Tourism Pvt. Ltd. runs one of Kerala's most trusted roller skating academies — training young champions through discipline, dedication and teamwork, on a smooth rink surface designed for both quad and inline skating.

Every skater follows a clear path from Beginner to Advanced, with individual coaching attention, full protective gear, and a training approach built around real competition — not just casual rink time.

Skating levels

Three levels. One path to mastery.

Our roller skating curriculum takes every skater from first push-off to competitive form, one milestone at a time.

Level 01

Beginner Skating

Balance, braking and turning — the fundamentals of roller skating, taught with full protective gear and one-on-one attention.

Level 02

Intermediate Skating

Speed, agility and strength drills build on the basics, preparing skaters for structured races and technical skating tests.

Level 03

Advanced & Competitive

Tournament-focused coaching — race strategy, endurance and championship-level technique for competitive skaters.
